Air fryer chicken wings

Description

Crispy chicken wings in half an hour with minimal mess.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken wings
  • 1/4 cup starch (corn, tapioca, potato... all work fine)
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • fresh ground black pepper to taste
  • Hot sauce or Korean wing sauce

Directions

  1. Remove chicken wings from package, if they are very wet drain til only moist.
  2. Scatter starch and seasonings over the wings and stir or shake to distribute well.
  3. Place on parchment in a preheated 400F air fryer and bake for 13 minutes.
  4. Turn wings over at 13 minutes and bake for another 10 minutes.
  5. Wings will be crispy but not necessarily brown. At this point, add hot sauce or wing sauce, mix well to coat, and serve hot.
